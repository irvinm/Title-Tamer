Import & Export

Safely backup your configurations or rapidly install custom Rule Packs from the community.

📦

Export Patterns

Generate a perfect snapshot of your current patterns, groups, and settings as a .json file.

  • Backups: Keep your rules safe before making major changes.
  • Sharing: Send your custom rules to friends or colleagues.
📥

Import Patterns

Load patterns from a .json file. After selecting a file, you can choose how they apply:

  • Append: Merges new rules safely. Identical duplicates are skipped.
    Best for installing Rule Packs.
  • Replace: Completely overwrites your current setup.
    Best for restoring backups.
